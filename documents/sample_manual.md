# Acme WidgetPro 3000 — Quick Reference

## Resetting the device
To reset the WidgetPro 3000, press and hold the **power button** for **10 seconds**
until the status LED blinks blue three times. The device will restart with factory
defaults. Your saved presets are erased by a reset, so export them first from
**Settings → Presets → Export**.

## Charging
The WidgetPro 3000 charges over USB-C. A full charge takes about **2 hours** and
lasts roughly **18 hours** of continuous use. A solid amber LED means charging; a
solid green LED means the battery is full.

## Connecting to Wi-Fi
Open **Settings → Network**, choose your network, and enter the password. The
device supports 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz networks. If the device cannot see your network,
make sure 5 GHz band steering is disabled on your router.

## Firmware updates
Firmware updates are delivered automatically over Wi-Fi. To check the current
version, go to **Settings → About**. To force an update check, tap the version
number five times.

## Troubleshooting
If the device becomes unresponsive and the reset does not help, leave it on the
charger for 30 minutes and try the 10-second power-button reset again. If the
status LED stays red, the unit needs service — contact support@acme.example.
