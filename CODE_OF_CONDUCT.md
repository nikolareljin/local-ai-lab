# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in `local-ai-lab` a
welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or
identity. We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open and healthy community.

## Our standards

We expect everyone in this community to:

- Be kind, patient, and respectful - especially toward newcomers learning the material.
- Welcome questions and assume good intent; offer and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the people learning from it.
- Take responsibility for mistakes, apologize to those affected, and learn from the experience.

Behavior that harms others - harassment, personal attacks, demeaning or discriminatory comments,
publishing others' private information, or other conduct that would reasonably be considered
inappropriate in a professional setting - is not tolerated.

## Adoption

This project adopts the **[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)**.
Please read the full text for the complete standards, enforcement guidelines, and the scope in
which they apply. The summary above is provided for convenience and does not replace it.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to the project maintainer at
**nikola.reljin@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and
the maintainer will respect the privacy and security of the reporter. Maintainers who do not follow
or enforce this Code of Conduct in good faith may face temporary or permanent repercussions as
determined by the project's leadership.
