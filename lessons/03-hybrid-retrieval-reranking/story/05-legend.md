# Chapter the Fifth — The Legend That Followed

The Ocean's Memory returned to Earth in 2150 carrying the news, the data, and the soil samples that
would launch a hundred new expeditions. Caretta came home a legend, greeted as the greatest
explorer in the history of two worlds.

She never spoke of glory. When the leaders of Earth offered her every honor, she asked only to be
returned to the tide pools of the Galápagos, to feel once more the ordinary tides of the world that
made her. And there she remains, sunning herself on the warm stones, listening to the gulls. In the
floating cities and the lunar colonies and the first settlements of Nuevo Edén, parents still tell their
children of Caretta the Magnificent, the magic turtle who became an astronaut, who navigated by the
song of the stars, and who found for all of us a second home among the heavens.
