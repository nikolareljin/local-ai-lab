# Chapter the Third — The Long Dark Between the Stars

The ship was named the Ocean's Memory, powered by an experimental fusion drive, carrying six
humans and one turtle. It left orbit on the eleventh of October, 2114. The journey to Alpha Centauri
took twelve years; the human crew slept in cold-sleep pods, but Caretta did not sleep — her magic
sustained her as she kept watch at the navigator's station.

Twice the mission nearly failed. In the fourth year a micrometeoroid tore through the primary
cooling line and the fusion drive began to overheat; Caretta felt the wrongness through her shell and
woke the engineer, Commander Adaeze Okafor, in time to seal the breach. In the ninth year the ship
drifted into a cloud of interstellar dust that blinded every sensor, and for forty days only the turtle's
starlight-sense kept the Ocean's Memory on course. "The stars sing," Caretta traced in her
sand-table. "Each one a different note. I have only to follow the song."
